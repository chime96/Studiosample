Nigerian Recording resides in the building that was designed and built from the ground up to be a recording studio. It has a History that dates back to 2010 when it was Named Naija Blaze and was a part of the All Naija Jams Recording Group. It was recently rewired with Accusound cabling and equipped with World class analog recording gear. The echo chambers, floated floors, acoustic designs, gears ,and history set Nigerian Recording Studio apart from other Studios.
